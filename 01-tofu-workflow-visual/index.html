10. MOFU — Middle of Funnel (Interested Lead Nurture)

30-90 Day Nurture for Leads That Don't Convert Immediately

Any lead that comes out of TOFU as "interested" but doesn't book a meeting enters MOFU. Same sub-segment strategy (I1/I2/I3) — just extended over a longer timeframe with multiple channels.

Lead exits TOFU as "Interested"Completed initial post-call sequence but didn't convert
Enters MOFU Nurture (30-90 days)Extended sequences based on their sub-segment
Email SequencesFortnightly or monthly drip
Content, insights, soft CTAs
LinkedIn TouchpointsProfile views, post engagement
Connection requests
Content DripsThought leadership, case studies
Market insights
Engagement signal firesReplies, clicks 3+ links, visits website
No engagement for 90 daysRe-engage or archive
Escalate to sales for personal follow-upOr move to BOFU signal tracking

MOFU by Sub-Segment

I1: Pain Point → MOFU

Monthly value content addressing their pain

  • Cadence: Monthly email + quarterly personal check-in
  • Channels: Email + LinkedIn engagement
  • Content: Pain-specific articles, case studies, market data
  • Exit: Replies or engages → back to sales
I2: Bad Timing → MOFU

Stay visible until their stated timeline

  • Cadence: Monthly content, resurface at stated timeline
  • Channels: Email + LinkedIn
  • Trigger: Funding round, job posting, or their stated "next quarter"
  • Exit: Timeline arrives → personal reach-out
I3: Wrong Person → MOFU

Try new angles to reach the decision-maker

  • Cadence: Monthly to original contact + new DM attempts
  • Channels: Email + LinkedIn direct to DM
  • Content: Exec-friendly summaries, different value angles
  • Exit: DM engages → sales takes over

11. BOFU — Bottom of Funnel (Discovery Stage Signal Tracking)

Live Signal Monitoring via Clay + Automation

Any lead at Discovery stage (had a call, considering TSP) enters BOFU. We track live signals for their company via Clay and automation — when a trigger fires, sales gets alerted to strike at exactly the right moment.

Lead at Discovery Stage in HubSpotHad a call, considering TSP, hasn't committed
Clay + Automation monitors LIVE SIGNALSFunding, hiring, leadership changes, growth, re-engagement
New fundingSeries A/B raised
Sales job postingBuilding sales team
New CRO/VP SalesLeadership change
Headcount jump20%+ growth
TSP re-engagementWebsite visit / LI
TRIGGER FIRESAutomated alert → sales person + HubSpot task created
Sales person follows up with timely, relevant message"Congrats on the raise — when we spoke in [month], you mentioned [X]..."
Goal: Turn "not right now" into "actually, let's talk" — at exactly the right moment. BOFU is about timing, not volume.
⚠️ KEY TO-DO: Define the signal list — which specific triggers do we track for companies at Discovery stage? This needs a session with the team. Start with the top 3-5 highest-value signals.
