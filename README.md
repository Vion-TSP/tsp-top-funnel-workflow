# TSP Marketing Workflow System

Three-layer interactive marketing workflow system for The Sales People's GTM engine. Each framework builds on the previous one — from test design to tech integration to campaign execution.

## Frameworks

### 1. [TOFU Workflow Visual](01-tofu-workflow-visual/) 
**The test plan.** Three parallel outbound tests (A/B/C) to determine whether LinkedIn ad warm-up improves cold outreach conversion. Includes:
- Overall test architecture (3,000 contacts split into 3 groups)
- Per-test timelines and flows
- Interested lead sub-segments (I1/I2/I3)
- SDR call tagging process
- LinkedIn ad creative mix
- MOFU nurture workflows (30-90 day)
- BOFU signal tracking
- Budget and measurement framework
- 51 tasks across 6 phases

### 2. [Unified Funnel Architecture](02-unified-funnel-architecture/)
**The tech stack integration.** How Clay, HubSpot, Instantly, Connect & Sell, LinkedIn Campaign Manager, and Slack connect across the full funnel. Includes:
- Clay command centre (enrichment, signals, 5 output feeds)
- TOFU tools: LinkedIn ads, Instantly cold email, Connect & Sell dialler
- HubSpot CRM as single source of truth
- MOFU 7-category nurture engine (A-G) with 171 existing leads mapped
- BOFU signal-based alerts
- 15 data flows mapped with integration status
- Blockers and dependencies

### 3. [LinkedIn Campaign Architecture](03-linkedin-campaign-architecture/)
**The campaign execution plan.** Account-based LinkedIn strategy across 3,099 companies in 4 campaigns, with every piece of content backed by Connect & Sell transcript data. Includes:
- 4 campaigns: Build From Zero (720 cos), Add Outbound (786), Optimise Engine (810), GTM Efficiency (783)
- 34 content assets: 8 TLAs, 3 video variants, 3 stat graphics, 20 organic posts, 2 PDF playbooks
- Full TLA post drafts in Sully's voice (all 8)
- Full organic post drafts (all 20)
- Video production briefs with scripts
- Case study & testimonial video strategy
- 8-week posting schedule
- LinkedIn URL merge utility script
